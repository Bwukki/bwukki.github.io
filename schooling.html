OSU Cascades

OSU Cascades is situated in Bend, OR and is a relatively new college with an even newer Computer Science program. They primarily focus on Web & Mobile Devlopment (And will offer internship oppurtunities through MECOP & their professional program), more advanced degree options are offered at their parent school: OSU.

While OSU Cascades does not offer a graduate program themselves, their parent school OSU offers both Masters and PHD level work. Degrees are offered specializing in Theoretical Comp Sci, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Systems, Programming Languages, Software Engineering, and Computer Vision and Graphics.

A particularly interesting area of research being done by OSU includes AI and robotics. Their research in AI has been used in forest fire management, species conservation, emergency response, and agriculture, while their robotics research has been used in making intelligent wheel chairs, ocean exploration, along with various medical and agricultural applications.
